# Mantis Hackathon Marketing Plan

## Goal

Keep the build visible without overposting.

The aim is to have enough public progress for the Bitget AI hackathon/community side, while keeping every post tied to something real: idea, build progress, results, dashboard, submission.

Use the official Bitget AI handle shown on the campaign page or X. In the current X screenshots this appears as `@Bitget_AI`. If the campaign page says a different handle, use that exact one.

Use `#BitgetHackathon` where it fits, especially on progress and submission posts.

---

## Posting Style

- all lowercase
- no polished startup voice
- no long explanations
- no periods unless the line really needs it
- blank lines between thoughts
- write like a builder talking in public, not a company account
- keep each post to one clear idea
